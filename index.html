About Me

I'm a researcher in X-Order Lab. Before coming to X-Order Lab, I was a researcher at Tencent WeChat. I obtained my master's degree from Institute of Computing Technology (ICT), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS) in 2018.

My general research interests cover the broad area of algorithms design and complexity theory, with special emphasis on online algorithms and combinatorial optimization. My current works focus on location problems.
